About Us

WasteNot is a company that specializes in collecting and recycling e-waste. We work hard to ensure that e-waste is properly disposed of and recycled, and we also resell some of the e-waste we collect to manufacturing companies to be repurposed. Join us in our mission to make the world a cleaner and greener place!
